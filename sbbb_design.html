Overview & Summary

Sky Blues Big Band is an extracurricular group of middle and high school students, organized and led by my younger brother Sam Barnes. For the past two years, I have recorded and edited videos of their holiday performances.

In 2022 I decided to create a logo and branding for the band, as well as other promotional marketing material that they needed. I started with the rectangular layout, and created the more compact circular version afterwards, which ended up fitting a lot better on the promotional flyers and prints.
